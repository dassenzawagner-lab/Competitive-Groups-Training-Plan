Block / Beam Drop Jumps

Jumping from a Block/Beam down (Both legs):

  • Forward

  • Sidewards (Left)

  • Sidewards (Right)

  • Backwards

Jumping from a Block/Beam down (Right leg):

  • Forward

  • Sidewards (Left)

  • Sidewards (Right)

  • Backwards

Jumping from a Block/Beam down (Left leg):

  • Forward

  • Sidewards (Left)

  • Sidewards (Right)

  • Backwards

Rotation Jumps

Jumping from a Block/Beam down (Both legs) + Half Turn:

  • Forward (Half turn each direction)

  • Sidewards (Half turn each direction)

  • Sidewards (Half turn each direction)

  • Backwards (Half turn each direction)

Jumping from a Block/Beam down (Both legs) + Full Turn:

  • Forward (Full turn each direction)

  • Sidewards (Full turn each direction)

  • Sidewards (Full turn each direction)

  • Backwards (Full turn each direction)
