Training Plan: Flexibility & Conditioning

Flexibility & Stretching


  • -Split Stretching (3 sets of 3 mins): Right, left, and center positions without weights or bands (seated/supported).

  • Wall Bar Leg Lifts (15x): Standing position (front, side, and back).

  • Floor Leg Lifts (30x): Lying on the floor (front, side, and back).

  • Wall Bar Split Stretch (30x): Each leg.

    Jumps & Explosivity

  • Trampoline Jumps (10x per set): Using weights and resistance bands. Includes split jumps (right & left), switch jumps, and straddle jumps.

  • Springboard Jumps (10x per set): Focusing on maximum reach/height.

  • Oversplit Holds (1 min): Each side.

  • Floor Jumps (5x per set): Same jumps as above, but on the floor without weights or bands.

    Strength & Conditioning( if time)

  • Push-ups: 15 reps.

  • Rope Climb: 2 times.

  • Handstand Press: 15 reps.

  • Pull-ups: 10 reps.

  • Wall Bar Leg Raises (Core): 20 reps.

  • Cross Sit-ups: 50 reps.
