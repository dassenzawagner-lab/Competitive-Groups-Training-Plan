Workout Plan Arms

Phase 1: 5 kg Disk/Plate Exercises


Complete 15 reps for each position while laying on the floor, lifting arms over head:

15x Laying on the belly

15x Laying on the side (Right)

15x Laying on the other side (Left)

15x Laying on the back


Phase 2: Dumbbell Exercises (5 kg in each hand)


20x Overhead press (Arms long, pressing shoulders up)

20x Front raises (Lifting arms up from hanging and slowly down)

20x Lateral raises (Lifting arms up sidewards and slowly down)

20x pullin up shoulders( Arms hanging down)


Phase 3: Bodyweight & Strength

15x Chin-ups

15x Decline Push-ups (Push-ups with legs higher)

15x Bars Dips

10x Bensch pusch ups(That thing that you can lay on)


Phase 4: Core & Back

10x (Each Side) Upper body lift while laying on the belly

Weight Circles (Finish with weight circles as indicated)
